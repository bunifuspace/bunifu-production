Who We Are

We are a movement called by God to share the eternal gospel of Jesus with every nation, tribe, language, and people. Our mission is preparing hearts for Jesus’ soon return. We help people discover the Bible’s life-changing truths for themselves, finding the freedom, healing, and hope that only Jesus offers.

Learn More

What We Believe

We believe in a God whose love never fails, even in a broken world. Despite the cosmic conflict that has raged since before time, God’s plan of salvation continues. Jesus is coming soon to restore our world to its original perfection. We await with joy the day when God will give us new hearts in a new earth, where we’ll live in His presence for eternity.

Learn More

Who We Are

Through our global network of churches, schools, hospitals, and humanitarian agencies, we strive to create glimpses of God’s coming kingdom in our present world. Following Jesus’ example, we serve others with compassion and love, finding Him in the faces of those we help. From community outreach to global missions, we extend Christ’s healing touch to a world in need.

Making Disciples Through the Everlasting Gospel

Our mission is to proclaim the everlasting gospel of Jesus Christ and prepare people for His soon return by leading them into a life of faith, obedience, service, and Christian discipleship. We fulfil this mission through four essential commitments.

Worship

Honouring God through reverent worship, heartfelt prayer, faithful Sabbath observance, and Bible-centred preaching.

Discipleship

Helping every believer grow spiritually through Bible study, Sabbath School, prayer, and Christian education.

Evangelism

Church Building Project

Building for Worship. Building for Mission.

The construction of our church is more than a building project—it is an investment in God's mission.

Our vision is to establish a welcoming place where future generations will worship, study God's Word, train young disciples, serve the community, and proclaim the everlasting gospel. The completed facility will include:

  • Main worship sanctuary
  • Sabbath School classrooms
  • Children's learning spaces
  • Youth fellowship hall
  • Prayer rooms
  • Administrative offices
  • Community service facilities
  • Accessible amenities for all
